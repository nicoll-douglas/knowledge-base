# Study Plan

Spend 30 minutes a day doing any one of the following activities:

- Shadowing
    - Watch a show or movie in Portuguese and repeat after the dialogue
    - Mimic their tone, rhythm and pronunciation as closely as possible
    - Use Lingopie or anything else
    - Write down any vocabulary, verbs, or phrases you don't understand

- Reading Out Loud
    - Read a book in Portuguese out loud
    - Make a note of any vocabulary, verbs or phrases you don't understand

- Journalling
    - Write a journal entry about a topic of your choice and your thoughts on it
    - To make it easier to select a topic, use a random topic generator
    - Make a note of any vocabulary, verbs, or phrases that you are trying to use that you don't already know

- Translation
    - Translate a song or a chapter of a book
    - This may be a song you know, the chapter of a book you are currently reading or have already read
    - Make a not of any vocabulary, verbs, or phrases that you didn't know

- Anki Cards
    - Any time you make a not of vocabulary, verbs or phrases you don't know also make an Anki card for it
    - Spend 15-30 minutes doing active recall with these Anki cards

- Conjugation
    - Spend 15-30 minutes conjugating a verb in your vocabulary bank into all tenses

- Studying
    - Spend 30 minutes doing exercises on Linguno
